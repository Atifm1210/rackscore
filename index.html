★ Rules

Tap a topic for the quick scoring guide.

◎ Objective

The goal is to pot balls in numerical order from 1 to 9 and finish with the highest score.

+ Scoring

Each ball is worth the same number as the ball.

  • Potting the 1-ball gives +1 point.
  • Potting the 2-ball gives +2 points.
  • Potting the 3-ball gives +3 points.
  • Continue this up to the 9-ball, which gives +9 points.
→ Order Rule

Players must always hit the lowest-numbered ball currently on the table first. If the 1-ball is next, the player must hit the 1-ball first. After it is cleared, the 2-ball becomes the next required ball.

! Fouls

A foul gives the player -3 points. A foul happens when:

  • The player hits the wrong ball first.
  • The player misses the required ball completely.
  • The player pots the cue ball.
○ Scratch Rule

If a player pots the correct ball but also pots the cue ball, the potted ball does not count and the player receives -3 points. Example: potting the 1-ball while scratching gives no +1; it gives -3 instead.

★ Winning

The game ends after the 9-ball is completed or when the match is finished. The player with the highest total score wins.
